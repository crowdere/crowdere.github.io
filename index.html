About

Boutique cybersecurity consultancy founded in 2016. We help small and mid-sized organizations get their security tooling right — properly configured, tuned, and working for your environment. No overselling, no shelfware.

Who We Serve

We work with growing businesses that have outpaced their security posture — usually a small IT team doing their best, with no dedicated security staff yet.

Is your IT or network team overburdened by security issues they weren't hired to handle?
Are you expanding internationally or opening new offices and unsure about the security implications?
Are your security tools not working as expected, generating too much noise, or sitting on the shelf?
Do you need security expertise but can't justify a full-time hire or a six-figure consulting engagement?

If any of these sound familiar, you're exactly who we built this practice for.

Services

Security Tool Configuration

Get the most out of the tools you already own. SIEM tuning, log source onboarding, endpoint agent rollouts — we make sure your stack is doing what it's supposed to.

MDR Deployment

Full MDR onboarding from scoping and agent rollout to integration and handoff. We make the transition smooth so you're not left figuring it out alone.

Detection Engineering

Custom detection content for your environment. We build and tune alert logic so your team spends less time on false positives and more time on what matters.

Post-Incident Recovery

If you've been hit with ransomware or a breach, we help you get back on your feet — rebuilding, hardening, and making sure it doesn't happen again.

Forward Deployed Analyst

Embedded security analysts who work directly alongside your team. Extra hands on deck when you need them, without the overhead of a full-time hire.

Virtual CISO

Strategic security leadership on a fractional basis. We help you build and maintain a security program, set priorities, and report to the board — without the executive price tag.
