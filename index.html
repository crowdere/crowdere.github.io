About

Boutique cybersecurity consultancy founded in 2016. We help small and mid-sized organizations get their security tooling right — properly configured, tuned, and working for your environment. No overselling, no shelfware.

Services

Security Tool Configuration

Get the most out of the tools you already own. SIEM tuning, log source onboarding, endpoint agent rollouts — we make sure your stack is doing what it's supposed to.

MDR Deployment

Full MDR onboarding from scoping and agent rollout to integration and handoff. We make the transition smooth so you're not left figuring it out alone.

Detection Engineering

Custom detection content for your environment. We build and tune alert logic so your team spends less time on false positives and more time on what matters.

Post-Incident Recovery

If you've been hit with ransomware or a breach, we help you get back on your feet — rebuilding, hardening, and making sure it doesn't happen again.

Right-Size Teaming

Every organization is different. We build a team around your specific needs by drawing from our extensive professional network of trusted security practitioners. Whether you need one person for a week or a small team for a quarter, we scale to fit — not the other way around.
